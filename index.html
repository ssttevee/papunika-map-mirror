How to use this map?

Enter zone: Left Click on zone/island icon
Leave zone: Right Click
Move to another zone: Click on the blue text near a portal to switch zones

Markers:
You can click on certain markers to get more information about it.

Hidden Stories:
If a hidden story has multiple parts, click on the marker to show the other locations. Example: #1 Hidden Story (1/3).

Cooking/Ingredients:
If cooking/ingredient items have multiple parts, click on the marker to show the other locations. Then craft at cook. Example: #1 Ingredient (1/2).
